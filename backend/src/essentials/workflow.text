Order process workflow (canonical order.status string on each line)

Source of truth — implementation: backend/src/modules/workflow/workflow.service.js (transitionOrderStatus)
Graph of allowed moves: backend/src/modules/workflow/workflow.transitions.js
Who may move: backend/src/modules/workflow/workflow.rules.js (departmentAllowsTransition + admin override)

1. Main lifecycle (human label → API status)

draft
   ↓ submitted
submitted
   ↓ sales_approved  (or on_hold, cancelled)
sales_approved
   ↓ finance_review
finance_review
   ↓ finance_approved | finance_rejected | on_hold
finance_rejected
   ↓ submitted | cancelled
finance_approved
   ↓ dispatch_pending
dispatch_pending
   ↓ dispatch_planned | on_hold
dispatch_planned
   ↓ partially_dispatched | dispatched
partially_dispatched
   ↓ partially_dispatched | dispatched
dispatched
   ↓ transport_assigned
transport_assigned
   ↓ in_transit
in_transit
   ↓ delivered
delivered
   ↓ invoice_generated
invoice_generated
   ↓ collection_pending
collection_pending
   ↓ partially_paid | paid
partially_paid
   ↓ paid
paid
   ↓ closed

on_hold may resume to: submitted, finance_review, dispatch_pending, or cancelled (department rules apply — see workflow.rules.js).

2. Transition side effects (same service)

- Same status → rejected (no-op).
- Allowed edge checked against workflow.transitions.js; department checked per target status (admin department always allowed).
- Finance move to finance_rejected requires non-empty rejection_reason (or remarks used as reason).
- Open OrderFlag with blocks_order=true may block the next status (see flag-blocking-rules.text / flagBlocksTransition).
- paid_amount may be supplied on transition; order payment_status and balance are recalculated (pending | partial | paid).
- Guards: partially_paid requires 0 < paid < grand_total; paid and closed require paid >= grand_total.
- When entering dispatch-related order statuses, order.dispatch_status is synced: pending, planned, partial, dispatched (Order model field — not the same string set as Dispatch document status).
- finance_approved / finance_rejected → OrderApproval row (finance_approval).
- invoice_generated → ensure Invoice exists for order.
- collection_pending → ensure Collection exists (after invoice).
- partially_paid / paid → update Invoice and Collection paid/collected fields and status.
- Always append OrderStatusHistory; write Activity (status_changed); notify (notificationService); recompute flag aggregates.
