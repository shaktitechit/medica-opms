7. Payment workflow — two concepts

(A) Payment records (payments collection)
Used for cheque / bank / ledger lines submitted against an order.

Document status enum (Mongo payment schema):

  submitted → verified | rejected

Finance verification should set verified_by / verified_at or rejection_reason on reject.

(B) Order-level money snapshot (same Order fields)
Workflow transitions drive business collection states:
  collection_pending ↔ partially_paid → paid → closed

Fields on Order:

- paid_amount — set via transitionOrderStatus({ paid_amount }) when moving into partially_paid / paid stages (and related guards).
- payment_status — derived on each transition by workflow.service.applyOrderPaymentDerived: pending | partial | paid (schema also allows rejected).

(C) Invoice (invoices collection) status enum

  draft | generated | partially_paid | paid | cancelled

Workflow updates invoice paid_amount/balance_amount when order hits partially_paid or paid — see transitionOrderStatus.

Rules:

- Partials allowed until grand_total met.
- Rejected Payment lines must carry actionable rejection_reason text.
- Order cannot reach closed until paid_amount ≥ grand_total (blocked in assertPaymentGuards).
